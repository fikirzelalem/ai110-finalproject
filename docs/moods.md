# Mood Guide

## Happy
Happy songs are uplifting, positive, and feel-good. High valence scores. Great for morning routines, social gatherings, or when you need a boost. Best genres: pop, indie pop, hip-hop.

## Chill
Chill songs are relaxed and low-stress without being sleepy. Perfect for studying, background listening, or unwinding. Best genres: lofi, ambient, folk.

## Intense
Intense songs are high-energy and emotionally charged. Great for workouts, gaming, or pushing through something hard. Best genres: rock, metal, electronic, hip-hop.

## Relaxed
Relaxed songs are slow, smooth, and easygoing. Lower energy than chill but warmer in tone. Great for evenings, reading, or winding down. Best genres: r&b, jazz, country.

## Moody
Moody songs are atmospheric and emotionally complex. Not sad, not happy — somewhere in between. Great for night drives, creative work, or introspective moments. Best genres: synthwave, electronic, indie pop.

## Focused
Focused songs help with concentration and deep work. Usually instrumental or low-lyric. Steady tempo and low distraction. Best genres: lofi, ambient, jazz.

## Euphoric
Euphoric songs feel like a rush. Very high energy and emotion. Best for celebrations, big moments, or peak workout intensity.

## Peaceful
Peaceful songs are gentle and calming. No sudden changes, smooth flow. Great for meditation or sleep.

## Aggressive
Aggressive songs are loud, fast, and intense. High BPM and distortion. Best for heavy workouts or releasing tension.

## Romantic
Romantic songs are warm, intimate, and emotional. Mid energy with expressive melodies. Best for date nights or sentimental moments.

## Nostalgic
Nostalgic songs have a wistful, bittersweet feel. Often slower tempo with warm instrumentation. Great for reflection.

## Dark
Dark songs have a brooding, atmospheric quality. Low valence, moody production. Great for late nights or creative focus.

## Dreamy
Dreamy songs feel floaty and ethereal. Low energy, spacious sound design. Great for zoning out or light sleep.

## Uplifting
Uplifting songs inspire and motivate without being aggressive. Positive energy with an emotional lift. Great for mornings or overcoming obstacles.

## Melancholic
Melancholic songs are sad but beautiful. Low energy, high emotional depth. Great for processing feelings or quiet evenings.
