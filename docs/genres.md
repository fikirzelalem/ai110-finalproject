# Genre Guide

## Pop
Pop music is upbeat, radio-friendly, and melody-driven. It works well for happy moods and social settings. High energy pop is great for working out or parties. Artists in the catalog: Neon Echo, Max Pulse, Indigo Parade.

## Lofi
Lofi hip-hop is mellow, repetitive, and low-energy. It is commonly used for studying, relaxing, or late-night coding. Usually acoustic-leaning with soft beats. Artists in the catalog: LoRoom, Paper Lanterns.

## Rock
Rock is guitar-driven and ranges from mid to very high energy. Intense rock works for workouts, gaming, or when you need motivation. Artists in the catalog: Voltline, Iron Veil.

## R&B
R&B is smooth, soulful, and emotional. It fits relaxed or romantic moods. Usually mid-energy with strong vocal melodies. Artists in the catalog: Velvet Tone, Soleil.

## Electronic
Electronic music is beat-heavy and produced. It covers a wide range from moody atmospheric to high-energy club sounds. Artists in the catalog: Circuit Nine, Prism Wave.

## Hip-Hop
Hip-hop is rhythm-focused with strong beats and lyrics. It tends to be high energy and great for dancing or hype moods. Artists in the catalog: Kraze.

## Jazz
Jazz is relaxed, improvisational, and sophisticated. It suits calm, focused, or nostalgic moods. Often acoustic. Artists in the catalog: Slow Stereo.

## Ambient
Ambient music is atmospheric and minimal. Very low energy, great for meditation, sleep, or background focus. Artists in the catalog: Orbit Bloom.

## Synthwave
Synthwave is retro electronic with 80s influences. Moody and cinematic, great for night drives or focused work. Artists in the catalog: Neon Echo.

## Metal
Metal is extremely high energy, aggressive, and intense. Guitar-heavy with fast tempos. Artists in the catalog: Iron Veil.

## Country
Country is storytelling-driven and often acoustic. Relaxed to mid energy, nostalgic feel. Artists in the catalog: The Willows.

## Folk
Folk is acoustic, introspective, and chill. Low energy with emotional depth. Artists in the catalog: Coastal Drift.

## Indie Pop
Indie pop blends pop melodies with alternative sensibilities. Upbeat but less polished than mainstream pop. Artists in the catalog: Indigo Parade.
